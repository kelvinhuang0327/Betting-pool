% ADR-0001: Wiki as Canonical Knowledge Layer

Decision: The `wiki/` directory is the canonical, normalized knowledge layer. Source markdown files remain in-place as artifacts.

Consequences:
- All architecture/design/guides are to be migrated or summarized into `wiki/`.
- Reports and generated outputs remain in `data/` or `archive/` and are referenced from the wiki.
- No runtime markdown is deleted or moved; this enables safe rollbacks.
